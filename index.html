Short story about me!

When i was younger I love capturing amazing moments with my polaroid camera whether it's a bird protecting it's chicks from a hawk or a kid running to her mother with excitement when she comes to pick her up.. i soon realized that every moments also has a story that needs to be told whether good or bad.
My love and inquisitiveness for telling stories with photography made me choose it as my main and i went ahead to study storytelling and photography at Harvard university and i went as far as getting a masters in it.
Ever since then I've worked companies like NATIONAL GEOGRAPHIC, United Nations, MTN telling amazing stories with photography and cinematography.
